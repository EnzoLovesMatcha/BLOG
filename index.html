Golden Pothos

Golden Pothos, scientifically known as Epipremnum aureum, is a popular and easy-to-care-for houseplant with heart-shaped leaves that are variegated in shades of green and yellow.

Alocasia

Alocasia cucullata, commonly known as Chinese Taro or Elephant Ear, is a visually striking tropical plant appreciated for its unique foliage and bold presence.

Palm tree

Palm trees, belonging to the family Arecaceae, are iconic symbols of tropical landscapes, renowned for their tall, slender trunks topped with a distinctive crown of feathery or fan-shaped leaves.

Monstera

Monstera deliciosa, commonly known as the Swiss Cheese Plant, is a popular and iconic tropical houseplant celebrated for its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves adorned with unique split patterns resembling Swiss cheese.

Boston fern

The Boston Fern, scientifically known as Nephrolepis exaltata, is a classic and graceful fern species prized for its delicate, feathery fronds that arch outward in an elegant manner.

Snake plant

The Snake Plant, scientifically known as Sansevieria trifasciata, is a resilient and popular indoor plant recognized for its striking upright leaves that resemble the patterns on a snake's skin.

Oyster plant

The Oyster Plant, scientifically known as Tradescantia spathacea, is a charming and easy-to-care-for perennial valued for its distinctive lance-shaped leaves and compact growth habit. Native to Mexico and Guatemala.

Rubber tree

The Rubber Tree, scientifically known as Ficus elastica, is a popular and resilient houseplant appreciated for its broad, glossy leaves and upright growth habit. Native to Southeast Asia.

Cactus

Cacti are a diverse and fascinating group of succulent plants well-known for their water-storing adaptations, spiky exteriors, and ability to thrive in arid environments.

Garden croton

Garden Croton, scientifically known as Codiaeum variegatum, is a vibrant and ornamental plant prized for its stunning, multicolored foliage and versatility in landscaping.

Anthurium

Anthurium, commonly known as the Flamingo Flower or Laceleaf, is an enchanting tropical plant celebrated for its distinctive, glossy heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, long-lasting spathe and spadix inflorescence.

Evergreen

The Chinese Evergreen, scientifically known as Aglaonema, is a popular and adaptable houseplant appreciated for its lush, attractive foliage and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
